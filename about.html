🌑 Dark Ascendant

About

An idle dark fantasy game, built in the browser.

Dark Ascendant is a free, idle-style dark fantasy game playable directly in your browser — no download, no account required. Tap to channel dark energy, recruit familiars, unlock forbidden rites, and rise through the ranks from a humble Noble Wanderer toward true ascension.

What is Dark Ascendant?

At its core, Dark Ascendant is an idle/incremental game: tap to generate dark essence, spend it on upgrades like grimoires, familiars, and ritual altars, and watch your power grow even while you're away. As you climb through levels, story moments unfold — your character's identity and title evolve from a wandering vagabond into something far darker.

Features

  • Idle progression with passive income while you're away
  • A tier system of evolving characters and titles, each with their own art and story
  • Boss encounters that test your accumulated power
  • A prestige ("Ascension") system for long-term replayability
  • Achievements, milestones, and a global leaderboard
  • Customizable visual themes
  • Accessibility options including adjustable text size, reduced motion, and haptic feedback toggles

Free to play

Dark Ascendant is free to play from start to finish. Optional Soul Gem packs and a one-time Remove Ads purchase are available to support development, but are never required to progress or enjoy the game.
